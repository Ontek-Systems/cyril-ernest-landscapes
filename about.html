The Story

A Family Legacy in Landscaping

With a passion for the great outdoors starting from a young age, founder Tony studied at a leading horticultural college and university to build a foundation of expert knowledge.

The company name, Cyril Ernest, holds deep personal significance. It was chosen in loving tribute to Tony's father, whom he sadly lost during his horticultural training. The business stands as an ongoing honor to his memory.

Now in our 30th year in the industry, Cyril Ernest Landscapes continues to provide a wide range of premium services to our clients, driven by a genuine passion for the sector and an unwavering commitment to excellence.
